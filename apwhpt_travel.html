Queues, Modelling, and Markov Chains: A Workshop Honouring Prof. Peter Taylor


Venue

The workshop will be held at the Eagle Heights Mountain Resort, Mount Tamborine, Queensland. The location of this resort is a popular place in Mount Tamborine with a walking distance to other accommodation options and restaurants. Information on the local area can be found at Visit Tamborine Mountain.


International travel information

  • All non-Australian citizens travelling to Australia require a visa.
  • Most New Zealand citizens with a valid New Zealand passport are granted an electronic Special Category Visa on arrival in Australia.
  • All other vistors need to obtain a visa prior to arrival to enter Australia.
  • Further information on the required travel documents can be found on the Australian border force website.
  • For details on vistor visa options see the Department of Home Affairs website.

Laser pointers and other prohibited items

Please be aware that laser pointers over 1 milliwatt power rating have been banned in Australia. Further details on this as well as information on other items that cannot be brought into Australia can be found on the Australian border force website.

Travel to and from Brisbane

  • Travel from Brisbane: Coach leaves Brisbane Friday 28 June 2pm (with stops at The University of Queensland, Roma Street Station, Beenleigh Station) to arrive at Eagle Heights around 4pm.
  • Travel to Brisbane: Coach leaves Eagle Heights Monday 1 July 11am (with stops at Beenleigh Station, Roma Street Station, The University of Queensland) to arrive in Brisbane around 1pm.
